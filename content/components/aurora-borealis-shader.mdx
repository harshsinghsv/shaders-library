## Features

- **Volumetric Raymarching**: 3D aurora simulation with depth and parallax
- **Curl Noise**: Swirling ribbon motion with divergence-free flow fields
- **Spectral Color Mapping**: Height and wavelength-based aurora colors
- **Atmospheric Scattering**: Realistic light scattering through atmosphere
- **Quality Settings**: Adjustable performance levels (low/medium/high)
- **Interactive Parallax**: Mouse-responsive camera movement

## Customization

Customize the aurora by adjusting:

- **Intensity**: Control overall brightness and visibility
- **Speed**: Adjust animation speed and flow rate
- **Quality**: Choose between low, medium, and high quality settings
- **Palette**: Customize color scheme with hex color arrays
- **Interactive**: Enable/disable mouse parallax effects

## Aurora Parameters

The shader includes multiple layers:
- **Primary Ribbons**: Large, flowing aurora curtains
- **Secondary Layers**: Additional depth and complexity
- **Turbulence**: Multi-scale noise for organic variation
- **Height Falloff**: Stronger aurora at mid-altitudes

## Color System

- **Green Spectrum**: `#00FFB3` - Primary aurora green
- **Blue Spectrum**: `#00A3FF` - Secondary blue tones
- **Purple Spectrum**: `#7B61FF` - High altitude colors
- **Pink Spectrum**: `#FF6EC7` - Rare pink aurora
- **Light Green**: `#9FFF6A` - Bright highlights

## Technical Details

- **Raymarching Steps**: 16-32 steps based on quality setting
- **Noise Functions**: 3D Simplex-like noise with FBM
- **Curl Noise**: Divergence-free flow field generation
- **Atmospheric Effects**: Exponential attenuation and fog
- **Tone Mapping**: ACES filmic tone mapping for cinematic look
