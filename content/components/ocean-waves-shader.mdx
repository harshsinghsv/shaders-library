## Features

- **Layered Waves**: Multiple wave frequencies for realistic ocean movement
- **Foam Effects**: White foam appears on wave peaks
- **Depth Gradient**: Color transitions from deep blue to light blue
- **Shimmer Effect**: Subtle light reflections on water surface

## Customization

Customize the ocean waves by adjusting:

- **Wave Height**: Control the amplitude of wave movement
- **Wave Speed**: Adjust individual wave layer speeds
- **Color Depth**: Modify the blue color gradients
- **Foam Intensity**: Control when and how foam appears

## Wave Parameters

The shader includes multiple wave layers:
- **Primary Waves**: Large, slow-moving base waves
- **Secondary Waves**: Medium frequency detail waves
- **Detail Waves**: High frequency surface ripples
- **Foam Layer**: Dynamic white caps on wave peaks

## Ocean Color System

- **Deep Blue**: `vec3(0.0, 0.1, 0.3)` for deep water
- **Light Blue**: `vec3(0.2, 0.6, 0.9)` for shallow areas
- **Foam White**: `vec3(0.9, 0.95, 1.0)` for wave peaks
